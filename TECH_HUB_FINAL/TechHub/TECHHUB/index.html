iPhone 14 Pro Summary

The iPhone 14 Pro was launched on September 16, 2022.

  • 6.12-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 2556 x 1179 pixels at a pixel density of 460 pixels per inch (ppi).
  • Powered by a hexa-core Apple A16 Bionic processor with 6GB of RAM.
  • Runs iOS 16 and is powered by a 3200mAh non-removable battery
  • Supports wireless charging, as well as proprietary fast charging.

Galaxy S23 | S23+

The Galaxy S23 was launched on 17th February, 2023.

  • 6.80-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1080 x 2340 pixels at a pixel density of 425 pixels per inch (ppi)
  • Powered by a Octa-core (1x3.36 GHz Cortex-X3 & 2x2.8 GHz Cortex-A715 & 2x2.8 GHz Cortex-A710 & 3x2.0 GHz Cortex-A510) with 8GB of RAM
  • Runs Android 13, One UI 5.1, and is powered by a 3900mAh battery and supports wireless charging.
